Hi, I’m Bianca, but you can call me Bibs (:

I’m a Product Design Leader with 10+ years of experience leading and scaling design across B2B, B2C, internal tools, and complex enterprise systems. I work where UX strategy, product direction, and delivery meet. Especially in environments that are messy, technical, and high-stakes.

I’ve led teams and products across Brazil, The Netherlands, India, and Germany, and I’m comfortable operating under real constraints: legacy systems, tight budgets, regulations, and low design maturity. I bring structure, prioritization, and clarity to help teams make better decisions and move faster.

My focus is outcomes over artifacts. I build design systems, processes, and teams that scale, reduce friction, and align closely with product and engineering. I do my best work when design is a strategic partner, shaping product direction and enabling organizations to ship better, more meaningful products.

What I Do

  • Product Strategy & Direction

    I help teams define clear product goals, success metrics, and priorities under real constraints. Through structured alignment with business, product, and engineering, I ensure strategic decisions are actionable and measurable.

  • Research Synthesis & Insight

    I translate qualitative and quantitative inputs into clear product signals. Rather than producing reports, I focus on extracting insights that reduce uncertainty and directly inform roadmap, scope, and prioritization.

  • Concept Framing & Trade-offs

    I support teams in exploring solution spaces while making trade-offs explicit. By balancing impact, feasibility, and effort, I help organizations decide what to build, what to defer, and what not to do.

  • Product Systems & Design Operations

    I design and evolve product design processes, governance models, and operating frameworks that scale. This includes defining roles, rituals, and standards that improve efficiency, consistency, and collaboration.

  • Validation & Decision Support

    I work with teams to validate assumptions through structured feedback loops, stakeholder alignment, and evidence-based discussions—supporting confident decisions without over-investing in execution.

  • Delivery Enabling & Follow-through

    I ensure strategic decisions translate into delivery by aligning teams, clarifying requirements, and supporting handover into development. My focus is on reducing friction, rework, and misalignment during execution.
