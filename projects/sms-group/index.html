SMS group GmbH

Global Head of Design

2021 - 2025

Scope: Europe & Latin America · 14+ Product Designers

Environment: B2B Industrial Software · Engineering-led · Legacy systems · Global scale

At SMS group, my role was to transform design from a fragmented delivery function into a scalable, outcome-oriented product capability operating across regions, products, and organizational silos.

Organizational Context

SMS group is a global industrial technology company with:

  • Highly complex software products
  • Strong engineering culture
  • Long product lifecycles
  • Legacy platforms and internal tools
  • Distributed teams across regions

The Challenge

  • Design practices varied widely between teams
  • UX involvement was inconsistent and often late
  • There was no shared product design process
  • Success was measured by output, not outcomes

This created friction, inefficiency, and limited the strategic impact of design.

My Mandate as Global Head of Design

I was accountable for:

  • Functional leadership of 14+ product designers across Europe and LATAM
  • Defining and scaling design strategy across the company
  • Implementing design governance and operating models
  • Managing budget and resource allocation
  • Embedding data-driven, outcome-oriented thinking beyond design

Over four years, I focused on three core outcomes:

  • People

    Developing designers into confident, autonomous product professionals

  • Structure

    Introducing OKRs, governance, and a result-oriented operating model

  • Delivery

    Designing and evolving a measurable Product Design Process that improved speed, clarity, and impact

This work resulted in measurable improvements in delivery efficiency, stakeholder satisfaction, and design maturity across the organization.

Leadership & Team Development

  • Developing designers as product professionals
  • Coached designers across seniority levels
  • Supported career progression and role clarity
  • Fostered autonomy, accountability, and confidence
Over four years, I saw 14+ designers grow into stronger product thinkers, leaders, and collaborators, capable of operating independently within complex environments.
This investment in people directly improved delivery quality and team resilience.

Cross-Functional & Data-Driven Impact

Championing a data-driven mindset

Design decisions were often challenged in an engineering-led organization.

What I did

  • Promoted data-informed decision-making beyond design
  • Introduced qualitative and quantitative feedback loops
  • Connected UX improvements to business outcomes

Outcome

  • 25% increate in stakeholder satisfaction, measured through regular feedback
  • Improved client perception during sales cycles
  • Positive movement in NPS and qualitative client feedback
Design became a credible contributor to business conversations.

Budget & Operational Responsibility

I was responsible for

  • Budget planning and ownership
  • Resource allocation across regions
  • Cost optimization

Outcome

  • Reduced operational costs
  • Maintained or improved quality
  • Ensured sustainable team scaling

Key Results

(4-Year Impact Summary)

  • 14+

    Led and developed product designers across two regions

  • 30%

    Increased design efficiency and delivery speed

  • 20%

    Improved team productivity

  • 25%

    Increased stakeholder satisfaction

  • OKR embedded and outcome-driven thinking across design

  • Established a scalable product design process and governance model

What I Learned

  • Design leadership at scale is primarily about systems and people, not screens
  • OKRs are powerful cultural tools when introduced with care
  • Governance enables speed when done collaboratively
  • Long-term impact comes from investing in people's growth
